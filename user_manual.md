# Creator User Manual - Engage AI

Welcome to Engage AI! This user manual guides you through using our AI YouTube Community Manager and Live Chat Assistant suite.

---

## 1. Onboarding and Authentication
1. Go to the dashboard and click **Login with Google**.
2. Complete onboarding:
   - Connect your YouTube channel.
   - Enter your email address and select timezone/language.
   - Pick your subscription plan (**Free**, **Pro**, or **Agency**).
3. Click **Complete Onboarding** to navigate to your dashboard.

---

## 2. Analyzing Video Comments Queue
1. Navigate to **Analyze Video** in the sidebar.
2. Enter your YouTube Video URL or Video ID into the analyzer bar and click **Analyze**.
3. Once crawled, you will see a detailed breakdown:
   - **Comments List**: Categorized into Questions, Praise, Feedback, and Spam.
   - **AI Reply Suggestions**: Toggle between **Short**, **Medium**, and **Long** options.
   - **Calibration Prompts**: Edit the draft text area. Clicking **Approve & Post** opens a rating modal to refine future prompts.

---

## 3. Real-Time Live Chat Manager
1. Navigate to **Live Chat AI** in the sidebar.
2. Select your active live stream title from the dropdown list.
3. Click **Connect Live Chat**.
4. Monitor live chat streams:
   - **Super Chats**: Highlighted with colored tags indicating payment value.
   - **AI Suggestions**: Click **One-Click Fill** to load suggested responses into your message input box.
   - **Quick Actions**: Hover over chat items to Pin, Delete, Timeout, or Ban users.

---

## 4. Analytics & AI Memory Tuning
- **Analytics**: Navigate to **Analytics** to view growth line charts, sentiment donuts, category distributions, and demographics. Click **CSV** or **Print PDF** to export reports.
- **AI Learning**: Navigate to **AI Learning** to view average rating stats, edit diff logs, and manage prompt memory constraints.
