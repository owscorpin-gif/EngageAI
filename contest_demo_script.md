# Contest Demo & Walkthrough Script - Engage AI

This script outlines the step-by-step walkthrough demonstration showing the features and value proposition of the Engage AI SaaS suite.

---

## Part 1: Authentication & Onboarding (Duration: 1.5 mins)
1. **Scene**: The homepage login screen.
2. **Action**: Click **Login with Google**. Show the smooth loading skeleton and redirect.
3. **Scene**: Onboarding setup screen.
4. **Action**: Connect the demo YouTube channel "WebDev Tutorials", choose "Pro Plan" (Recommended), and click **Complete Onboarding**.

---

## Part 2: Dashboard Overview & Analytics (Duration: 2 mins)
1. **Scene**: Primary Creator Dashboard.
2. **Action**: View KPI cards (Total Comments, AI Replies rate, Pending Reviews).
3. **Action**: Click **Analytics** in the sidebar. Highlight:
   - The responsive SVG Growth curves (toggling Daily/Weekly/Monthly view).
   - Donut charts displaying Sentiment breakdown.
   - Demographics row showing language percentage.
4. **Action**: Click **Export CSV** (downloads spreadsheets report) and **Print PDF** (shows print layout).

---

## Part 3: Comment Intelligence & AI Reply Customizer (Duration: 2.5 mins)
1. **Scene**: Analyze Video panel.
2. **Action**: Paste a mock YouTube URL and click **Analyze**. Show the progress bar.
3. **Action**: Once processed, show comment classification tabs:
   - Click "Questions" -> view tech queries.
   - Click "Spam" -> show muted Bitcoin links.
4. **Action**: Toggle draft sizes: click **Short**, **Medium**, and **Long** to see length variations.
5. **Action**: Edit a draft, changing the tone. Click **Approve & Post**.
6. **Action**: The **AI Calibration Dialog** pops up. Rate the draft 4 stars, select "Too formal", and click **Submit & Post**.
7. **Action**: Navigate to **AI Learning** tab. Show the correction entry and the newly generated prompt memory constraint.

---

## Part 4: Live Stream Chat AI Assistant (Duration: 2 mins)
1. **Scene**: Live Chat AI panel.
2. **Action**: Choose "React 19 Q&A Stream" and click **Connect Live Chat**.
3. **Action**: Observe live comments streaming:
   - Highlight Super Chat cards ($19.99 and $49.99).
   - Point to the AI Suggested Replies under viewer queries.
   - Hover over a comment and click **Pin** (displays in pinned header banner) or **Timeout/Delete**.
4. **Action**: Click **One-Click Fill** on an AI suggested reply, filling the chat input, and hit send.

---

## Part 5: Admin Oversight (Duration: 1 min)
1. **Scene**: Admin Control Panel.
2. **Action**: Go to **Flags & Alerts**, publish broadcast warning: *"Engine maintenance scheduled at 2:00 AM PST"*.
3. **Action**: Highlight the amber warning banner immediately appearing at the top of the dashboard.
